Our philosophy

Our aim is it to provide contemporary, high quality and functional dental solutions to all our patients. Using the latest technologies and the cooperation with the most renowned partners in this industry has the highest priority in the interests of our patients. One of our closest collaboration partner is the company GC Germany – a well known international manufacturer for dental products and dental materials in dental technology as well dentistry. We, MB Dentaltechnik, contribute within the industry by being part of developing and improving new GC products until there are ready for the dental market.
Because of our global advances and high quality of work can we proudly look back to a long relationship and wonderful teamwork with our dentists. All in the service to satisfy our patients.
Our young and dynamic team masters every dental challenge in an individual and professional way, giving always their best.
The key to success in our company lies in the satisfaction of our patients, to whom we are fully and permanently committed.

Courses we offer

In our facilities of MB Dentaltechnik in Düsseldorf, Ralf Dahl and Michael Brüsch offer regularly advanced training courses, including master courses in composite, metal ceramic and all-ceramic. The hands-on seminars provide theoretical and practical options how to achieve a functional, prosthetically solution using GC Gradia and GC Initial. On different substructures will be different techniques used to make restorations. In addition, external trainers and speakers are invited for advanced trainings.
